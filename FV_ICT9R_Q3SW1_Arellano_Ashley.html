Sypnosis:

Cha Hyun-Su, A high school student living alone after his family died in a car accident tries to survive the apocalypse together with the residents of his apartment building. Based on the Webtoon comic by Kim Kan-Bi & Hwang Young-Chan.

Cha Hyun-Su

The main protagonist of the story. He was a regular high school student before his parents and little sister died in a car accident. After their death, he moved into the Green Home Mansion Apartment where he was trapped in by monsters of the apocalypse.

Lee Eun-Hyeok

A former medical student who gave up his studies and career to take care of his little sister, Lee Eun-Yu after their parents demise. During the apocalypse, he was the acting leader of the survivors.

Seo Yi-Kyung

A firefighter before the apocalypse, Seo Yi-Kyung was of service to the community. She was engaged to her fiancee as they were residing in Green Home Mansion Apartment. However, things get ruined as her fiancee goes missing and turns to be an infected monster.
